quadrilaterals

A square is defined as a plane figure with four equal sides and four right angles. A rectangle is defined as a plane figure with four straight sides and four right angles, especially one with unequal adjacent sides, in contrast to a square. A kite is defined as a quadrilateral figure having two pairs of equal adjacent sides, symmetrical only about its diagonals. A rhombus is defined as a parallelogram with opposite equal acute angles, opposite equal obtuse angles, and four equal sides.
